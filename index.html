For over 100 years we’ve been providing world-class mobile guest experiences to our customers. From manufacturing equipment for the emerging film industry in the 1920s, to providing the hotel industry the first housekeeping carts in the 1950s, Forbes has continued to provide innovative solutions that make our customers successful. Today, we’re known for manufacturing mobile equipment and portable furniture designed to help our customers increase their guests’ satisfaction.

Forbes brings that same commitment to providing our customers an extensive line of high-quality, linenless tables perfect for meetings & events. These products are manufactured in the USA and are trusted by the largest hotels in the world. These Meetings & Events tables and other portable products are unrivaled in terms of safety, reliability, and overall value. At Forbes Industries, we take pride in the quality of our work!
